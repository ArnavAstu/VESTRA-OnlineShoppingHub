Frequently Asked Questions

What is VESTRA?

VESTRA is a fashion e-commerce platform founded by Arnav Astu and Ishan Kumar offering trendy clothing at affordable prices.

How long does delivery take?

Orders are delivered within 3–7 working days depending on your location.

Can I return a product?

Yes, products can be returned within 7 days of delivery.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order from your Profile → Orders section.
